Ashley Podsiad-Sharp

Ashley Podsiad-Sharp was initially arrested for sharing rap music online that would 'inspire terrorism'. He was found not guilty of this ridiculous offence, but the police raided his home and took all his digital devices as they were clearly desperate to find something else to convict him on. Sadly, they managed to dig up a 3 year old text document 'that would be useful to a terrorist'. His defence were able to prove he'd neither opened nor read the document but the jury found him guilty of 'possession' anyway.

Facility: HMP Swaleside
Release Date: 05/23/2031
